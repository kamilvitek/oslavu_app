## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies allow a website to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

Cookies can be either "session cookies" or "persistent cookies." Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted.
